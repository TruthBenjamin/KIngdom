Frequently asked questions

Is there a service fee?

Kingdom Marketplace is currently free for buyers and sellers while the platform grows.

How do I choose the right creator?

Start with category fit, then compare examples, reviews, response time, and how clearly they answer your project brief.

How do payments work?

The current beta payment system supports protected marketplace workflow testing. It is not connected to a live payment provider yet.

Can I hire for ongoing work?

Yes. Many creators can support retainers, recurring media needs, and long-term ministry or business projects.
