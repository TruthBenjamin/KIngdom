Terms of Service

These terms describe how Kingdom Marketplace accounts, services, hiring, messaging, reviews, payments, and moderation work during public beta.

1. Accounts and eligibility

You may create an account as a buyer, seller, or both. You are responsible for keeping your login details secure, providing accurate account information, and using Kingdom Marketplace only for lawful marketplace activity.

2. Buyer and seller roles

Buyers can browse, save, message, hire, and review sellers. Sellers can activate creator tools, complete a profile, list services, receive messages, manage orders, and submit services for review before they appear publicly.

3. Service listings

Listings must be accurate, lawful, respectful, and owned or authorized by the seller. We may approve, reject, pause, remove, or request edits to listings that are incomplete, misleading, unsafe, infringing, or outside the purpose of the marketplace.

4. Orders, scope, and delivery

A buyer hires a seller by booking a service and completing checkout. Requirements, scope changes, delivery files, revision requests, cancellation requests, and disputes should stay inside Kingdom Marketplace so the order record is clear.

5. Payments and beta records

Payment, refund, withdrawal, wallet, fee, and adjustment tools may be beta records unless a live payment provider is connected and clearly shown during checkout. Do not treat simulated beta balances as withdrawable funds.

6. Messages, files, and content

You are responsible for the messages, files, profile details, listings, reviews, and other content you submit. Do not upload harmful files, unlawful material, private information you are not allowed to share, or content that violates another person's rights.

7. Reviews and marketplace trust

Reviews should come from completed orders and reflect the buyer's actual experience. We may hide, flag, or remove reviews that are abusive, misleading, spammy, retaliatory, unrelated to the order, or otherwise harmful to marketplace trust.

8. Moderation and safety

We may verify sellers, review reports, keep audit records, restrict accounts, pause marketplace activity, or remove content when needed to protect users, enforce these terms, comply with law, or preserve platform reliability.

9. User conduct

Use the platform respectfully. Do not harass users, impersonate others, evade platform safeguards, manipulate orders or reviews, misuse reports, attempt unauthorized access, or move users off-platform to avoid marketplace protections.

10. Changes and contact

We may update these terms as the marketplace grows. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised terms. Questions about these terms can be sent through the contact page.

These terms are designed for the current Kingdom Marketplace beta and are not a substitute for legal advice. For questions, contact us through the contact page.
